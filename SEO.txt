1. Write descriptive and concise text for your <title> elements. Avoid vague descriptors like "Home" for your home page, or "Profile" for a specific person's profile.
2. <meta name="description"> tag from a page to generate a snippet in search results. Identical or similar descriptions on every page of a site aren't helpful when individual pages appear in search results.
3. Sitelinks best practices: At the moment, sitelinks are automated. Make sure that the text you use as your page titles and in your headings is informative, relevant, and compact.Create a logical site structure that is easy for users to navigate, and make sure you link to your important pages from other relevant pages. Ensure that your internal links' anchor text is concise and relevant to the page they're pointing to. Avoid repetitions in your content.
